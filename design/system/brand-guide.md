# Flamingo Florist Brand Guide

## Brand Identity

### Mission Statement
We are Flamingo Florist. With 30 years of experience in weddings, parties, events, and funerals, we create custom floral arrangements that touch hearts and celebrate life's special moments. We believe everyone is special and deserves unique, personalized designs that reflect their story.

### Brand Promise
"We don't create cookie-cutter arrangements. We craft works of art that make people smile, cry happy tears, and feel truly special."

### Core Values
- **Personal Touch**: Every arrangement is custom-made with love and attention to detail
- **Emotional Connection**: We understand the power of flowers to convey feelings and create memories
- **Artistry**: We are artists who happen to work with flowers, not an assembly line
- **Listening**: We take time to understand our customers' visions and stories
- **Quality**: 30 years of experience ensuring excellence in every creation

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Flamingo Pink**: #FF6B9D (vibrant, joyful, signature color)
- **Coral Sunset**: #FF8A80 (warm, welcoming, elegant)
- **Dusty Rose**: #E8A598 (soft, romantic, sophisticated)

**Secondary Colors:**
- **Sage Green**: #87A96B (natural, fresh, botanical)
- **Cream**: #F8F6F0 (clean, pure, elegant background)
- **Soft Gold**: #D4A574 (luxury, warmth, special occasions)

**Accent Colors:**
- **Deep Navy**: #2C3E50 (trust, stability, elegance)
- **Warm Gray**: #8B8680 (neutral, sophisticated)

### Typography

**Primary Font (Headings):**
- Elegant serif font (like Playfair Display)
- Used for main headings, hero text, and special quotes
- Conveys elegance and sophistication

**Secondary Font (Body):**
- Clean sans-serif font (like Inter or Poppins)
- Used for body text, navigation, and forms
- Ensures readability and modern feel

### Visual Elements

**Signature Elements:**
- Flamingo silhouettes and illustrations
- Watercolor flower elements
- Rhinestone sparkle accents
- Elegant bow details
- Hand-drawn flourishes

**Photography Style:**
- Real work showcase (never stock photos)
- Emotional moments (smiles, tears of joy)
- Behind-the-scenes artistry
- Before/after transformations
- Customer reaction shots

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Warm & Personal**: Like talking to a dear friend
- **Passionate**: Genuine love for the craft shines through
- **Storytelling**: Every arrangement has a story
- **Encouraging**: Helping customers envision their dreams
- **Heartfelt**: Emotional connection in every interaction

### Tone Guidelines
- Use "we" and "you" for personal connection
- Share stories and memories
- Be encouraging and supportive
- Celebrate uniqueness
- Show genuine care for customers' needs

### Example Phrases
- "Let's create something magical together"
- "Every flower tells a story"
- "Your vision, our artistry"
- "Made with love, delivered with care"
- "30 years of making moments special"

## Design Principles

### Layout & Composition
- **Flowing & Organic**: Mimic natural flower arrangements
- **Elegant White Space**: Let elements breathe
- **Emotional Hierarchy**: Lead with feelings, follow with facts
- **Personal Touch**: Include handwritten elements
- **Warm Welcome**: Inviting, not intimidating

### Interactive Elements
- **Soft Animations**: Gentle, flower-like movements
- **Warm Hovers**: Subtle color transitions
- **Storytelling Scrolls**: Reveal content like unfolding petals
- **Personal Calls-to-Action**: "Tell us your story" not "Buy now"

## Content Strategy

### Key Messages
1. **Custom Artistry**: "We create works of art, not cookie-cutter arrangements"
2. **Emotional Connection**: "Flowers that make you smile, cry happy tears, and feel special"
3. **30 Years Experience**: "Three decades of creating magical moments"
4. **Personal Service**: "We listen, we care, we create your vision"
5. **Local Artistry**: "Supporting local with locally-sourced beauty"

### Content Types
- **Story-driven**: Customer testimonials and reaction stories
- **Behind-the-scenes**: Artistry process and care
- **Seasonal Features**: Custom seasonal arrangements
- **Education**: Flower care and selection guidance
- **Gallery**: Real work showcase, not stock photos

## User Experience Goals

### Primary Objectives
1. **Emotional Connection**: Visitors feel the warmth and care
2. **Trust Building**: Showcase real work and experience
3. **Story Sharing**: Encourage customers to share their vision
4. **Call Inspiration**: Make visitors want to call and connect
5. **Uniqueness**: Stand out from corporate flower services

### Success Metrics
- Increased phone consultations
- More wedding/event bookings
- Higher customer satisfaction
- Stronger local presence
- Repeat customer growth

## Brand Applications

### Website Elements
- Hero sections with real customer stories
- Gallery showcasing actual work
- Flamingo elements as decorative accents
- Uplifting quotes and sayings
- Personal consultation call-to-actions

### Marketing Materials
- Business cards with flamingo watermarks
- Brochures showing real arrangements
- Social media with behind-the-scenes content
- Email signatures with personal touches
- Seasonal promotions with custom themes
